Mental Masturbation
Consuming self-improvement content is the most addictive form of doing nothing.
Tap to flip
What it means
Most of what people call self-improvement is just consumption with better branding. You feel sharper after a podcast, more capable after a book — but nothing in your life has changed. Information goes in. Behavior stays the same. You confuse the input with the outcome.
Analogy
Like buying a gym membership, watching workout videos every morning, reading the science of hypertrophy — and never lifting a single weight.
Reflect & use it
Look at what you've consumed this month. Now look at what you've done. Which one is closer to the person you say you are?
